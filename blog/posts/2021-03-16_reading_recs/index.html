Network Science Reading Recs

Published

March 16, 2021

This is a list of network science books curated by some fine folks on Twitter. Note that some of these aren’t quite network science related but interesting nonetheless! This isn’t an exhaustive list so feel free to email me any other suggestions to add!

  • Networks, Crowds, and Markets by Kleinberg and Easley
  • Understanding Social Networks: Theories, Concepts, and Findings by Kadushin
  • The Development of Social Network Analysis: A Study in the Sociology of Science by Freeman
  • Mathematics of Epidemics on Networks by Kiss, Miller, and Simon
  • Fundamentals of Brain Network Analysis by Fornito, Zalesky, and Bullmore
  • The Atlas for the Aspiring Network Scientist by Coscia
  • The Human Network: How Your Social Position Determines Your Power, Beliefs, and Behaviors by Jackson
  • Complex Networks: Principles, Methods and Applications by Latora, Nicosia, and Russo
  • Network Science by Barabasi
  • Graph Spectra for Complex Networks by Van Mieghem
  • Networked: The New Social Operating System by Rainie and Wellman
  • Networks by Newman
  • A First Course in Network Theory by Estrada and Knight
  • The Formula: The Universal Laws of Success by Barabasi
  • The Origins of Order: Self-Organization and Selection in Evolution by Kauffman
  • Networks: A Very Short Introduction by Caldarelli and Catanzaro
  • Quantum Techniques for Stochastic Mechanics by Baez and Biamonte
  • A First Course in Network Science by Menczer, Fortunato, and Davis
  • Dynamical Processes on Complex Networks by Barrat, Barth‌élemy, and Vespignani
  • Evolution of networks by Dorogovtsev and Mendes
  • Random graphs and complex networks by van der Hofstad
  • Complex Networks: Structure, Robustness and Function by Cohen and Havlin
  • Scale-Free Networks: Complex Webs in Nature and Technology by Caldarelli
  • Probabilistic Foundations of Statistical Network Analysis by Crane
  • Social Network Analysis: Methods and Applications by Wasserman and Faust (Cool Visualization)
  • Distance In Graphs by Buckley and Harary
  • Complex Graphs and Networks by Chung and Lu
  • Six Degrees: The Science of a Connected Age by Watts
  • Small Worlds: The Dynamics of Networks between Order and Randomness by Watts
  • Gathering Social Network Data by adams
